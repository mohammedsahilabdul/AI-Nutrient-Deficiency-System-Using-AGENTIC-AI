## 🎓 Recommended Reading Order for Evaluators

**If I were the evaluator, I would read in this order:**

1. **First (5 min):** EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md

   - Sees key metrics and achievements
   
2. **Second (15 min):** Skim INTERIM_PROJECT_REPORT.md Sections 1-4
   - Understands architecture and design
   - Sees technical complexity
   
3. **Third (10 min):** Review PROJECT_DIAGRAMS_FIGURES.md
   - Visualizes system components

4. **Fourth (10 min):** Deep dive INTERIM_PROJECT_REPORT.md Sections 6-7
   - Sees test results and quality
   - Reviews challenges and solutions
   
5. **Finally (5 min):** Appendix of INTERIM_PROJECT_REPORT.md
   - Confirms technical stack and deliverables
